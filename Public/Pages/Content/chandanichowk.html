CHANDANI CHOWK

Chandni Chowk is a world of its own! While the rest of Delhi is spurring up with new cafes and bars, the age-old restaurants and modest stalls/kiosks found at Chandni Chowk are just as precious. We took a spin of some of the most famous and least explored places in that part of town and here’s what we found! Also, some of the places might be closed given the current scenario, so make sure you check up on ‘em before you plan on heading out.

Giani Di Hatti
This place is one of the oldest restaurants in the neighbourhood and apart from delish food, they do these absolutely decadent Rabri Faluda. A glass of this delight includes soft faluda topped with creamy rabri, which is then thoroughly mixed together! Tempting, right?! Pair it up with their legendary chole bhature and you’ll be sorted for the rest of the day.
Where | Giani Di Hatti - 651/52, Fatehpuri, Church Mission Road Timings | 8:30 AM - 11 PM Call Them | +91 11 2393 6174 Meal For Two | Rs 150 2. Annapurna Bhandar article image Run by the 3rd generation of sweet makers, this century-old sweet shop churns out the most authentic Bengali desserts in all of Delhi. The shop offers a wide-ranging menu right from the ultra spongy Rasgullas to the lesser-known exquisite desserts like Kadambari. You have got to savour their eclectic Misti Doi & Sandesh on your next trip to Chandni Chowk. Where | Annapurna Bhandar - Near Sheesh Ganj Gurudwara, Chandni Chowk, Timings | 8 AM - 7 PM Call Them | +91 11 2396 2050 Meal For Two | Rs 100 3. Chaina Ram Sindhi Halwai article image Picture Credits: khaata_rahe_mera_dil This 120 years old shop is amongst the most iconic sweet shops in Fatehpuri Chowk. The Malai Ghevar and Karachi Halwa here are probably the best you can bag in the city. After all, you don’t get to see people making a beeline for a sweet shop even on non-festive days, do you? Drop by for some lip-smacking Samosas, Besan Ladoos and piping hot jalebis, on a cold winter morning! Where | Chaina Ram Sindhi Halwai - Fathepuri Chowk, Chandni Chowk Timings | 8 AM - 8:30 PM Call Them | +91 11 2392 7585 Meal For Two | Rs 350 4. Kanwarji’s article image Picture Credits: foodandcaptures People from all over the country visit Kanwarji’s to saviour their mouth-watering Dal Biji, whose recipe is a closely guarded family secret. Other sweets and namkeens which you have got to try out here are their spectacular Pista Lauj, Aloo Ke Lachhe, Chocolate Ghewar & Chana Barfi amongst others. They also do yummy chole bhature which are a crowd fave! Head on over for your share and you can thank us later. Where | Kanwarji’s - Corner Gali Paranthe, Opposite Central Bank, Chandni Chowk Timings | 9 AM - 10 PM Call Them | +91 8802 219 883 Meal For Two | Rs 300 5. Meghraj & Sons article image A trip to Meghraj & Sons means goodness in the form of lots and lots of sweet dishes. While their kalakand and barfis are amazing, it's the lassi which makes us go back to this eatery again and again. Some of the must-try dishes here include Paneer Bread Pakoda, Kaju Halwa, Chocolate Barfi and more! Where | Meghraj & Sons - Fateh Puri Chowk, Chandni Chowk Timings | 7:30 AM - 9 PM Call Them | +91 11 6535 7876 Meal For Two | Rs 200 6. Bhaja Govindam article image Picture Credits: rohya8 Bhaja Govindam is a family restaurant that serves flavoursome South as well as North Indian cuisines. Their Kaju Korma, Mysore Masala Dosa and a vibrant Idli Platter make for an ideal feast after a movie session at Delite Cinema. Sounds like a plan to us! Troop on over and make the most of the Delhi winter. Where | Bhaja Govindam - Ground Floor, Delite Theatre Building, Asaf Ali Road, Daryaganj Timings | 11 AM - 11:30 PM Call Them | +91 9643 911 813 Meal For Two | Rs 500 7. Shyam Sweets article image Picture Credits: khaugalifoodie The legendary Shyam Sweets is known for their breakfast treats and sweets since 1910 and they have a killer combo that you must try. Get your hands on the breakfast-special Bedmi Aloo, Nagori Halwa and Cham Cham and jumpstart your taste buds and your foodie trail right! The piping-hot spicy aloo sabzi along with the bedmi, flavourful aachar and salad are a match made in heaven and perfected by Shyam Sweets. Combine that with the Nagori Halwa and Cham Cham here and we’re sure you’ll be able to hear the angels sing! As it’s always swamped by people including foreign tourists, we suggest that you reach here early and avoid the crowds. Where | Shyam Sweets - 112, Barshahbulla Chowk, Near Metro Station, Chawri Bazar Timings | 8 AM - 10 PM Call Them | +91 11 2326 8087 Meal For Two | Rs 150 8. Kake Di Hatti article image Picture Credits: /foodie_.indian Kake Di Hatti is perhaps the most iconic dhaba of Delhi and it lives up to its reputation! If you think you have had the best Dal Makhani and Stuffed Naans, wait till you get floored by theirs. Saying that their naans are huge would be a gross understatement - you are defo in for a surprise!. Despite being an all veg North Indian restaurant, it never fails to pull a large crowd - a testimony to its ah-mazing taste and surprisingly cheap rates. Where | Kake Di Hati - Church Mission Rd, Cloth Market, Khari Baoli, Chandni Chowk Timings | 8 - 1 AM Call Them | +91 11 4355 2754 Meal For Two | Rs 500 9. Shiv Misthan Bhandar article image Picture Credits: Subir Kumar We won’t be lying when we say that we are absolutely in awe of Bedmi Poori here at Shiv Misthan Bhandar. Combine this with their signature Nagori Halwa and some jalebis, and you are defo in for a treat! They also serve yummy Pista Badam Lassi, Bedmi Aloo, Chole Bhature, and more. Where | Shiv Misthan Bhandar - Kucha Ghasi Ram, Chandni Chowk Timings | 8:30 AM - 9 PM Call Them | +91 11 2392 1406 Meal For Two | Rs 150 10. Tewari Brothers Confectioners article image Tewari Brothers is a little shop which whips up delightful snacks and sweets. It’s their quick service which makes them stand apart from other restaurants. Their Motichoor Laddoo, Raj Kachori, Samosa and Dosa should definitely be on your list. Take along your BFFs and pig out on toothsome dishes. Where | Tewari Brothers - Katra Nawab, Chandni Chowk Timings | 8 AM - 9 PM Call Them | +91 11 2395 6846 Meal For Two | Rs 300 11. Haldiram's article image Picture Credits: chatore_broothers Haldiram’s needs no introduction, as it’s perhaps the most popular chain of multi-cuisine family restaurants in the whole country. One of the largest restaurants in Chandni Chowk, with a huge sitting area, you can drop by here to savour your favourite comfort foods, including Raj Kachori, Onion Kachori, Chole Bhature, Dahi Papdi, Kaju Katli and more.
