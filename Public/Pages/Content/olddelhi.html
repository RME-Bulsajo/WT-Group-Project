OLD DELHI

1. Qureshi Kebab Corner

Qureshi Kebab Corner is one of the most popular street food shops in Old Delhi that serve juicy, tender and savoury kebabs. Qureshi Kebab Corner was started by Haji Abdul Ghani Qureshi per his grandfather, who sold kebabs on a stall near Jama Masjid. The place is currently run by five sons of the founder on the recipe handed down by the father. The place is forever bustling with guests, so you might have to wait a little to savour the little treats.
Must Try Dishes: Seekh kebab, Chicken tikka, Chicken malai tikka
Address: Urdu Bazar Rd, Meena Bazaar, Kalan Mehal, Old Delhi, New Delhi
Timings: 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM

2. Natraj Dahi Bhalla

Natraj Dahi Bhalla serves the best Dahi Bhalla in Delhi - juicy, sweet and sour. Served with a generous helping of dahi, a dash of sweet sonth chatni and a wisp of savoury green chilly chutney, the dahi bhallas are a bomb of flavours. They only have two items on their menu from their inception in 1940 until today: Dahi Bhalla and Aloo Tikki served with sauce. Both of these are worth a taste when you are here.
Must Try Dishes: Dahi Bhalla, Aloo Tikki
Address: 1396, Main Road Beside Central Bank, Kucha Mahajani, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi
Timings: 11:30 AM - 9:00 PM

3. Daulat ki Chaat, Chandni Chowk

Who doesn't know about the ever so popular Daulat ki Chaat? Amazingly enough, the chaat is a sweet offering made from creamy, frothy milk. Enriched with a generous helping of khoya, sprinkled with saffron and adorned with a rose, one teaspoon of this treat is known to melt in the mouth instantaneously. The preparation of this delicacy takes 6 hours and is freshly served in the morning. You can find it on several mobile stalls near Kinari Bazaar.
Must Try Dishes: Daulat ki Chaat
Address: Near Jain Temple, Kinari Bazar, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi
Timings: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM

4. Old Famous Jalebi Wala

Old Famous Jalebi Wala is one of the most famous sweet shops and street food spot in Old Delhi known for its fat and juicy jalebis. The jalebis are filled with sweet sugary syrup and are served with a dollop of freshly made rabri. The delicacy is especially savoured in the winters; however, the shop is crowded even during summers. Opened in 1884, these jalebis are fried in homemade desi ghee and are the talk of the town.
Must Try Dishes: Old Famous Jalebi Wala
Address: 1795, Dariba Kalan Rd, Opp Central Baptist Church, Dariba Kalan, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi
Timings: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

5. Lotan Ke Chole Kulche

Lotan ke Chole Kulche in Old Delhi is known for its hot and spicy flavoured chole served with the soft and bland kulche and a twig of green chilly and some salad. People come all across from town to relish this delicacy. Been in service since 1977, the chole kulche are famous even today. And this establishment is forever surrounded by a crowded. So if you are in Old Delhi anytime, don’t forget to try this out.
Must Try Dishes: Chole Kulche
Address: 2368, Chatta Shahji Rd, Manohar Market, Nai Wala, Old Delhi, New Delhi
Timings: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM

6. Karim’s Kebabs

Karim’s is a household name, not just in Delhi but in several other cities of the country. Although the place is a proper eating joint known for proper dinner and lunch sittings, we couldn’t refrain from mentioning it in the street food category due to its delectable kebabs, chicken tikkas, etc. Murg malai and other starters. The tiny restaurant is always buzzing with people, so you might have to wait for 15 - 20 minutes if you decide to visit at the peak hours.
Must Try Dishes: Chicken Kebabs, Chicken Tikka, Chicken Roasted, Murg Barra
Address: 16, Gali Kababian, Jama Masjid, New Delhi
Timings: 9:00 AM - 12:30 AM

7. Jung Bahadur Kachori Wala

You all must be familiar with the round puffy balls of fried dough filled with chopped onion and spices, popularly called Kachori. And who doesn’t savour a crispy spicy kachori served with delicious gravy. To taste some of this, you can head out to Jung Bahadur Kachori Wala in Chandni Chowk, the shop known to serve some of the best kachoris in town ever for ages. This snack is usually served with some tamarind and mint chutney which adds to the flavour. The menu only has kachoris on it - either full plate or half.
Must Try Dishes: Kachoris
Address: 1104, Gali Bhojpura Rd, Maliwara, Katra Lehswan, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi
Timings: 10:30 AM - 7:30 PM, Closed on Sunday

8. Paranthe Wali Gali

Paranthe Wali Gali is this entire lane in Chandni Chowk that serves all kinds of stuffed parathas. Joints after joints stuck in the walls like a closet, sell mouth-watering parathas stuffed with all possible mixtures that you can ever think of- from aloo to gobhi, chilly, cheese, keema, egg, paneer and mooli etc. The shops started to operate around 1870 during the Mughal reign, and the oldest one of them is Pandit Gaya Prasad Paranthe Wala.
Must Try Dishes: Paneer Paratha, Chilly Paratha, Aloo Paratha
Address: Katra Lehswan, Chandni Chowk, Delhi
Timings: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM

9. Sita Ram Diwan Chand Chole Bhature

Snuck in one of the cosy bylanes of Old Delhi, Sita Ram Diwan Chand Chole Bhature is the pride of Old Delhi. Although it might take some time for you to figure out the shop, the taste of the food will be totally worth it. They serve only in the early hours of the morning and finish by early afternoon. The chole are soaked overnight and marinated well before being cooked. And the cooked dish is served with tender and soft bhature which are relished by one and everyone.
Must Try Dishes: Chole Bhature
Address: 2243, Rajguru Marg, Chuna Mandi, Paharganj, New Delhi
Timings: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

10. Shiv Misthan Bhandar

Shiv Mishthan Bhandar is known for its all-day breakfast of bedmi poori, aloo poori and samosa chaat. These puffy bedmi pooris are the highlight of the sweet house, which are served with spiced and curried potatoes. The other speciality to try out at the Shiv Misthan Bhandar is Nagori Halwa, known to melt in the mouth. Suji ka Halwa and yummy lassi are available here, other than all the other regular sweetmeats and treats.
Must Try Dishes: Bedmi Poori, Nagori Halwa, Aloo Poori, Lassi
Address: 375, Chandni Chowk Rd, Kucha Ghasiram, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi
Timings: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
